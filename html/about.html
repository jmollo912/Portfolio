About me.

Growing up in South Philadelphia, I was born into a life of sports. Some of my fondest memories are playing football in the snow with my friends, surrounded by a city that lives and breathes competition. I like to think that upbringing shaped who I am as a UX designer, because at its core, sports is about solving problems on the fly. That’s exactly what UX design demands, and it’s something I’ve been doing my whole life.

Being in UX is not as simple as creating a beautiful hi-fidelity wireframe. Through the process of creating something not only attractive but accessible, I’ve developed a real appreciation for the design process.
