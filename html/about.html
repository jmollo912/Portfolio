Hey, I'm Giuseppe 👋

I am a UX/UI Designer with a graphic design background. I love creating intuitive user experiences that flat out just make sense.

My approach to design is rooted in understanding people — their needs, frustrations, and goals. I believe that great design should feel invisible, guiding users naturally through their journey without friction.

My Journey

Growing up, I was always fascinated by how things worked and how they could be made better. This curiosity led me to explore various creative outlets, from sketching ideas in notebooks to building things with my hands.

My passion for design emerged naturally as I discovered the intersection of creativity and problem-solving. I found that design wasn't just about making things look good — it was about making them work better for people.

Through my education and experiences, I've developed a deep appreciation for the design process, from initial research and ideation to prototyping and final execution. Each project teaches me something new and reinforces my commitment to creating meaningful experiences.
